**Abstract:**  
This paper proposes a trajectory tracking control method for a four-wheeled independent drive and steering AGV. The control strategy combines **LSTM-based Model Predictive Control (LSTM-MPC)** for lateral motion prediction and **Fuzzy PID** for longitudinal decoupling, achieving high-precision tracking and strong robustness to nonlinearities.
